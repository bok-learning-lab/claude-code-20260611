# General options 

Option 1. Expectation of complete avoidance of generative AI for out of class work on the grounds that it will be harmful to the accomplishment of the learning goals for the assessment. However, as a ban is not directly enforceable, the enforced policy will be that students will be expected to have full responsibility and a comprehensive understanding of the work that they submit.

Option 2. Generative AI use permitted in ways specified with an expectation of citation or other record of how it was used. Expectation of responsibility for and comprehensive understanding of work submitted. 

Option 3. Generative AI use permitted without reservation. 

# Clarification of Assignment-Specific Variations

Policy should say how and where students will be notified about permitted generative AI use if AI use will be permitted on some or all assignments. 

# Enforcement

Policies should indicate that if follow up assignments, discrepancies in student work, or other factors cause instructors to believe that the policy may have been violated, students may be asked to speak with instructors about their work and may be referred to the Honor Council.

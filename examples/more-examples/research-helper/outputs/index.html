    Context Rot: How Increasing Input Tokens Impacts LLM Performance

  • 02
    Lost in the Middle: How Language Models Use Long Contexts
    A U-shaped curve: models use information best at the start or end of the input, worst in the middle. (Liu et al. · arXiv:2307.03172 / TACL · 2023)
  • 03
    Context Length Alone Hurts LLM Performance Despite Perfect Retrieval
    Longer input degrades reasoning even when retrieval is perfect; a "recite-then-solve" trick partly recovers it. (Du, Tian et al. · arXiv:2510.05381 · 2025)
  • 14
    Make Your LLM Fully Utilize the Context
    IN2 training builds data that forces use of information anywhere in context, fixing lost-in-the-middle in a 7B model. (An et al. · arXiv:2404.16811 · 2024)

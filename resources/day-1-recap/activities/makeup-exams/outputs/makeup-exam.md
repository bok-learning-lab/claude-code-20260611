## Equation Sheet

| Quantity | Formula | Variables |
|---------|---------|-----------|
| Fick's First Law | J = −D (dC/dx) | J = flux, D = diffusion coefficient, dC/dx = concentration gradient |
| Random walk (1D) | x = √(2Dt) | x = average displacement, D = diffusion coefficient, t = time |
| Thermal diffusion timescale | τ = R²/α | R = length scale, α = thermal diffusivity |
| Thermal diffusivity | α = k / (ρ·cp) | k = thermal conductivity, ρ = density, cp = specific heat |
| Viscous shear stress | τ = η · (du/dy) | η = dynamic viscosity, du/dy = velocity gradient (shear rate) |

**Constants:** 1 m = 100 cm = 1000 mm = 10⁶ μm | 1 mPa·s = 10⁻³ Pa·s
